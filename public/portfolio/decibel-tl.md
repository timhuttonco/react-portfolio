### Technical Lead at Decibel
#### Nov 2014 - Jun 2016

Decibel’s AI scores every online customer experience to expose what is really happening on your website, and provides the most advanced tools available to understand why, including <a href="https://decibel.com/session-replay/" target="_blank" title="Decibel Session Replays">Session Replays</a>, <a href="https://decibel.com/heatmaps/" target="_blank" title="Decibel Heatmaps">Heatmaps</a> and <a href="https://decibel.com/capabilities-form-analytics/" target="_blank" title="Decibel Form Analytics">Form Analytics</a>.

I was hired as one of the first employees at Decibel, and the first Technical Lead, which involved the creation of <a href="../portfolio/decibel-support" target="_blank" title="Decibel Support Processes">support processes</a> that are still in place to this day.

What started as a one-person support team eventually grew to an 18-person Service Delivery organisation operating in London, Madrid and Denver, USA, incorporating <a href="../portfolio/decibel-support" target="_blank" title="Decibel Support Processes">technical support</a>, <a href="../portfolio/decibel-implementation" target="_blank" title="Decibel Implementation Processes">implementation</a>, pre-sales and technical account management functions, as Decibel continued to expand and raise <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2017/05/03/decibel-insight/" title="Decibel Insight scores $9M Series A for ‘digital experience analytics’ tech" target="_blank">$9m in Series A funding in 2017</a> and <a href="https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20191121005453/en/Decibel-Raises-17-Million-in-Series-B-Funding-Round" title="Decibel Raises $17 Million in Series B Funding Round" target="_blank">$17m in Series B funding in 2019</a>.

Tasks and highlights included:
* Dealing with, debugging and resolving issues raised by clients
* Individually creating very first public facing documentation in the form of the <a href="../portfolio/decibel-knowledgebase" target="_blank" title="Decibel Knowledgebase">Decibel Knowledgebase</a>
* Supporting new clients with on-boarding
* Creating first <a href="../portfolio/decibel-support" target="_blank" title="Decibel Support Processes">support processes</a> within Service Delivery team
* Assisting sales team with technical queries and attending relevant sales pitches, both remote and on-site, where technical knowledge is required
* Training and being point of escalation for new Technical Support Analysts
* Using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and browser consoles on a daily basis
* Responsible for all internal documentation
* Escalating relevant issues using <a href="https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira" target="_blank" title="JIRA">JIRA</a> and working closely with engineering team
* Providing suggestions to product team based on client feedback and usage
