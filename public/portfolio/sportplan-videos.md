### Sportplan: Video Tutorials

As part of continuous improvements to the Sportplan website and platform, a project was undertaken to revamp the help section available to users. This included how-to content in the form of written and video content.

Video tutorials on all key features of the Sportplan platform were created using <a href="https://www.techsmith.com/video-editor.html" title="Camtasia" target="_blank">Camtasia</a>. Updated support content went on to decrease the number of support requests created, along with improving the first-time usage of features of the platform.
