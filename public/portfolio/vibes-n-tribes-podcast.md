### The Vibes 'N' Tribes Podcast

<a href="https://linktr.ee/vibesntribes_" target="_blank" title="The Vibes 'N' Tribes Podcast">The Vibes 'N' Tribes Podcast</a>, hosted by Bayo Adeoshun, started in September 2021 and describes itself as Diary of a CEO vibes, but for normal people with high aspirations.

In March 2023, I had the pleasure of being invited on by Bayo. In the episode, we discussed my journey to becoming <a href="../portfolio/ynap-analytics-implementation" target="_blank" title="">Senior Manager, Analytics Implementation</a> at <a href="https://www.ynap.com/" target="_blank" title="YOOX Net-A-Porter">YOOX Net-A-Porter</a>. 

We discussed the differences between full-stack, front and backend developers, what actually goes into coding and great resources for anyone looking to get into the tech industry.

The full episode can be watched on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GTD31OB7B7U" target="_blank" title="Episode 26 - Analytics, Devs & more with Tim Hutton">YouTube</a> below, or listened to on <a href="https://podcasts.apple.com/podcast/id1584840914?app=podcast&at=1000lHKX&ct=linktree_http&i=1000606313686&itscg=30200&itsct=lt_p&ls=1&mt=2" target="_blank" title="Episode 26 - Analytics, Devs & more with Tim Hutton">Apple Podcasts</a> and <a href="https://open.spotify.com/episode/32iiuASYfaGLCMgoxUq24O" target="_blank" title="Episode 26 - Analytics, Devs & more with Tim Hutton">Spotify</a>.
