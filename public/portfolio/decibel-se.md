### Solutions Engineering at Decibel
#### Jun 2016 - Aug 2018

Decibel’s AI scores every online customer experience to expose what is really happening on your website, and provides the most advanced tools available to understand why.

Following the successful growth of Decibel and the Service Delivery team both in the U.K., U.S., and eventually Madrid, I was promoted as the first Solutions Engineer in the U.K. office. The main purpose of the role was to ensure the successful <a href="../portfolio/decibel-implementation" target="_blank" title="Decibel Implementation Processes">on-boarding and implementation</a> of our U.K. and EMEA clients while also being an escalation point for <a href="../portfolio/decibel-support" target="_blank" title="Decibel Support Processes">Technical Support Analysts</a>.

Later promoted to Senior Solutions Engineer, I was responsible for the <a href="../portfolio/decibel-implementation" target="_blank" title="Decibel Implementation Processes">on-boarding</a> of our largest U.K. and EMEA clients, while also managing all members of the U.K. Service Delivery team, which includes other Solutions Engineers and <a href="../portfolio/decibel-support" target="_blank" title="Decibel Support Processes">Technical Support Analysts</a>. 

During this period, Decibel raised <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2017/05/03/decibel-insight/" title="Decibel Insight scores $9M Series A for ‘digital experience analytics’ tech" target="_blank">$9m in Series A funding in 2017</a> and were also named <a href="https://startups.co.uk/startups-awards/tech-business-of-the-year-2017/" title="Tech Business of the Year 2017" target="_blank">Tech Business of the Year</a> at the <a href="https://startups.co.uk/startups-awards/winners/" title="Startups Awards" target="_blank">Startups Awards in 2017</a>.

From a commercial view, the role also included sales responsibilities such as presenting at sales demos, leading Proof of Concepts, and contributing towards RFPs for prospective clients. 

In addition, I worked closely with client’s security teams along with contributing towards company processes in preparation for <a href="https://gdpr-info.eu/" target="_blank" title="GDPR">GDPR</a>.
