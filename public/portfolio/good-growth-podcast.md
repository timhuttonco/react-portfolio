### The Good Growth Podcast

<a href="https://goodgrowth.co.uk/" target="_blank" title="Good Growth">Good Growth</a> are a data and technology business who help global businesses realise the digital commerce failings that are holding them back, and to enact change and significant growth.

<a href="https://goodgrowth.co.uk/insights/podcasts/" target="_blank" title="The Good Growth Podcast">The Good Growth Podcast</a> is hosted by George Hall as he discusses the latest trends and developments in digital with senior leaders from a range of sectors.

In December 2022, I was invited onto the podcast to speak with George about a number of topics, resulting in two podcast episodes.

Part 1 covered how eCom brands fared in 2022, the importance of data, and what the future may hold for Analytics Teams and can be listened to below or on Spotify <a href="https://open.spotify.com/episode/3m6qhItgTBwwr4x80KF5YY?si=687a9db0183e43a9&nd=1" target="_blank" title="The Good Growth Podcast - Part 1: Tim Hutton on eCommerce, Data and Google">here</a>.
